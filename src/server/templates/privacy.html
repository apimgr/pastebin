What we collect

When you create a paste, we store its content, title, language, visibility setting, expiry time, view count, and a SHA-256 hash of your delete token. We do not collect or store IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or personal identifiers.

What we do not collect

  • No user accounts or authentication data
  • No cookies beyond any set by your browser for HTTPS
  • No tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or third-party telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers

Data retention

Pastes are deleted automatically when they expire or reach their burn-after view limit. You can delete your paste at any time using your delete token. Unlisted pastes are not shown in public listings but remain accessible via their direct link.

Public data

All public pastes are visible to anyone who visits this service. Do not paste sensitive data such as passwords, tokens, or private keys.

Contact

For questions or concerns, use our contact page.
